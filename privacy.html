Website Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 11, 2026.

Overview

Changing Places Consignment Shop ("Changing Places," "we," "our," or "us") uses this website to share store information, consignment details, recent highlights, location information, and contact options. It does not offer checkout, account registration, or payment collection.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you visit or interact with this website. This website is intended for general audiences and is not designed to collect personal information directly from website visitors.

Information You Choose To Send

This website includes contact options such as phone, text, and email links. When you use those links, your device may open your phone, messaging, or email app.

Any information you choose to send through phone, text, email, or another communication channel is sent by you to Changing Places through that channel. This may include your name, contact information, message contents, photos, or any other information you choose to provide.

Website And Hosting Information

Like most websites, this site and its hosting providers may automatically process basic technical information when the website is loaded.

This may include information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring page, timestamps, and error or security logs. This information is used to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the website.

Third-Party Services

This website embeds or links to third-party services, including Google Maps, Instagram/LightWidget, Facebook, Google Fonts, and the SimpleConsign consignor portal.

These third-party services may receive technical information such as your IP address, browser details, device information, referrer, and interaction data when their content loads or when you follow links to their websites or services. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Changing Places does not control how these third-party services collect, use, or store information.

SimpleConsign Consignor Portal

This website may link to the SimpleConsign consignor portal for consignor-related access. Information entered into or handled through the SimpleConsign portal is processed through that separate service and is subject to SimpleConsign's own privacy practices and terms.

Cookies

The core website does not set its own marketing cookies.

The Couch Dash game uses your browser's local storage to remember your high score, sound preference, and selected radio station on your device. This information is not used to identify or track you.

Embedded third-party services may set cookies or use similar technologies when their content loads or when you follow links to their websites. You can manage cookies and similar technologies through your browser settings and privacy tools.

Your Choices

You can limit some third-party data collection by using browser privacy tools, blocking or disabling third-party cookies, or adjusting your device and browser settings.

You can also choose to contact Changing Places directly by phone, text, or email instead of interacting with embedded map or social content.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates are made, the "Last updated" date will be revised.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to ChangingPlacesDSM@gmail.com.
